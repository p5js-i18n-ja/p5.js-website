description: >
  <p>指定された半径の球を描画します。</p>

  <p></p>

  <p>DetailX および detailY は、球の x 次元および y
  次元の細分割数を決定します。細分割が多いほど、球は滑らかに見えます。推奨される最大値は24です。24より大きい値を使用すると、警告が表示されるか、ブラウザーが遅くなる可能性があります。</p>


  <hr/>

  <p>Draws a sphere.</p>

  <p>A sphere is a 3D shape with triangular faces that connect to form a round

  surface. Spheres with few faces look like crystals. Spheres with many faces

  have smooth surfaces and look like balls.</p>

  <p>The first parameter, <code>radius</code>, is optional. If a
  <code>Number</code> is passed, as in

  <code>sphere(20)</code>, it sets the radius of the sphere. By default,
  <code>radius</code> is 50.</p>

  <p>The second parameter, <code>detailX</code>, is also optional. If a
  <code>Number</code> is passed,

  as in <code>sphere(20, 5)</code>, it sets the number of triangle subdivisions
  to use

  along the x-axis. All 3D shapes are made by connecting triangles to form

  their surfaces. By default, <code>detailX</code> is 24.</p>

  <p>The third parameter, <code>detailY</code>, is also optional. If a
  <code>Number</code> is passed,

  as in <code>sphere(20, 5, 2)</code>, it sets the number of triangle
  subdivisions to

  use along the y-axis. All 3D shapes are made by connecting triangles to

  form their surfaces. By default, <code>detailY</code> is 16.</p>

  <p>Note: <code>sphere()</code> can only be used in WebGL mode.</p>
